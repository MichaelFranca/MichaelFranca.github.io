This has resulted in Miami becoming a popular upset pick among college basketball analysts (and just about everybody else filling out a bracket), but what is it about Loyola that makes them so threatening? Well, in this article we’ll focus on their offense. The Ramblers didn’t get to this point through luck. The team's cohesion is evident in many ways, but most of all through their assist numbers.
